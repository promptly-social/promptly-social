Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Important: This Privacy Policy explains how Promptly collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our AI-powered LinkedIn content generation service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

  • Email address and name when you create an account
  • Profile information you provide (bio, topics of interest, websites)
  • Payment information (processed securely by our payment processors)

1.2 LinkedIn Integration Data

When you connect your LinkedIn account, we collect:

  • Your LinkedIn profile information (name, headline, summary, work experience)
  • Your LinkedIn posts and content you've published
  • Posts you've interacted with (liked, commented on) for content preference analysis
  • Your LinkedIn connections' public information for engagement analysis
  • OAuth tokens to enable posting on your behalf

1.3 Content Sources

  • Substack newsletter subscriptions you choose to connect
  • Website URLs you provide for content inspiration
  • Content from these sources that we analyze for topic relevance

1.4 AI Analysis Data

  • Writing style analysis based on your LinkedIn posts
  • Topics of interest derived from your content and interactions
  • Generated content suggestions and your feedback on them
  • Content performance metrics and engagement data

1.5 Technical Information

  • Device information, browser type, and operating system
  • IP address and location data
  • Usage analytics and feature interaction data
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Service Delivery

  • Content Generation: Analyze your writing style to create authentic LinkedIn posts
  • Personalization: Tailor content suggestions based on your interests and sources
  • Publishing: Post content to LinkedIn on your behalf when authorized
  • Performance Tracking: Monitor engagement to improve content quality

2.2 AI Processing

  • Process your LinkedIn content through AI models to understand your voice
  • Analyze content from your subscribed sources for relevance
  • Generate personalized post suggestions using machine learning
  • Continuously improve our AI based on aggregated user feedback

2.3 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance our platform
  • Conduct A/B testing for feature improvements
  • Provide customer support and resolve technical issues
  • Send important service updates and security notifications

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We share information with trusted partners who help us operate our service:

  • AI Processing: OpenRouter/OpenAI for content generation (data is not used for training)
  • Database Services: Supabase for secure data storage
  • Authentication: LinkedIn OAuth for account connections
  • Payment Processing: Stripe for subscription billing
  • Analytics: Privacy-focused analytics providers

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes or government requests
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Prevent fraud or security threats
  • Enforce our Terms of Service

3.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of our company, user information may be transferred as part of the transaction. We will notify users of any such change.

3.4 What We Don't Share

  • We never sell your personal information to third parties
  • We don't share your content with other users without permission
  • Your AI-generated content remains private until you choose to publish it

4. Data Security

4.1 Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Secure database storage with access controls
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Employee access limited to necessary personnel only
  • Multi-factor authentication for administrative access

4.2 Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active
  • LinkedIn content: Stored for AI analysis and improvement
  • Generated content: Retained for service functionality
  • Analytics data: Aggregated and anonymized after 2 years
  • Deleted accounts: All personal data removed within 30 days

5. Your Privacy Rights

5.1 Access and Control

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your information

5.2 LinkedIn Integration Control

  • Revoke LinkedIn access at any time through your account settings
  • Control which LinkedIn data we can access
  • Disable automatic posting while keeping content generation
  • Delete stored LinkedIn content from our systems

5.3 Communication Preferences

  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Choose which service notifications to receive
  • Control frequency of content suggestion notifications
